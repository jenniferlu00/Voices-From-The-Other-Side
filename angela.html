Name: Angela Bitney

Origin: Perth, Australia

Age: 25

Born and raised in Perth, Australia, Bitney came to New York 4 years ago to pursue her degree in performing art at the American Musical and Dramatic Academy. She now works as a barista at an Australian Cafe in East Village. Bitney talked about her hometown, her family, her experience with catcalling in New York City and how it had affected her.
