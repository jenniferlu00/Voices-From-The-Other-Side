Name: Esther Woo

Origin: Dallas, TX.

Age: 20

Born in Oklahoma, raised in Dallas, Esther came to New York two years ago to pursue her undergrad degrees in Art and Psychology. As a first generation Korean American, she noticed the difference between a typical Korean family and her family. Also the difference between a typical Dallas family and her family. Woo talked about her parents and how the female and male role played in her family.
