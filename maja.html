Name: Maja Parker

Origin: Slagelse, Denmark

Age: 27

Born and raised in Slagelse, Denmark, Maja first came to U.S. in 2010 and worked as a nanny for almost 2 years. In 2013, she started to pursue her degree in performing art at the American Musical and Dramatic Academy. She was married in 2014 and now works as a keyholder at a retail store. She talked about her parents, gender equality in Denmark and how the way she was brought up affected the way she integrated into the American society.
