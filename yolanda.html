Name: Yolanda Trejo

Origin: Caracas, Venezuela

Age: 24

Born and raised in Caracas, Venezuela, Trejo came to New York about a week and a half ago to work on her English and also live the full American experience. She graduated Andres Bello Catholic University with a degree in Civil Engineering. She now goes to school for English during the day and works at a Venezuelan restuarant at night. Trejo talked about her parents, her career in civil engineering, the political crisis in Venezuela and how it paved the uprising of a new wave of feminist movement.
